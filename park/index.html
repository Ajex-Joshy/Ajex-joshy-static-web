Forget the hassle of traditional pay-to-park systems. With ParkPlus, you can book your spot through our smart app on your phone or car dashboard, making payments seamless and convenient.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does ParkPlus determine parking rates?

ParkPlus uses real-time data from Google Maps API to analyze traffic demand, vehicle type, and parking duration, adjusting rates accordingly.

Can I book parking through the app?

Yes, ParkPlus offers a user-friendly app for booking parking spaces conveniently on your phone or car dashboard.

What payment methods are accepted?

ParkPlus supports cashless payments through FASTag technology, ensuring a seamless and secure transaction process.

How does ParkPlus ensure security of vehicles?

ParkPlus utilizes drone surveillance and A.I. technology to monitor parking spots, ensuring the safety and security of vehicles at all times.

Is dynamic pricing beneficial for users?

Dynamic pricing in ParkPlus offers users the advantage of lower charges during off-peak hours and fair rates based on demand, providing a cost-effective parking solution.
